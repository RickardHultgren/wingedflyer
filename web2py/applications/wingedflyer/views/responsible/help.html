Welcome to the {{=context_name}} Portal

This system allows you to manage participants and track their progress through execution signals and instructions.

Key Features:
  • Dashboard: Overview of all your participants and their status
  • Participant Management: Create, edit, and monitor participants
  • Instructions: Send messages and receive responses from participants
  • Execution Signals: Track daily progress reports from participants
  • Work Activities: Monitor what participants are working on
Getting Started:
  1. Create participant accounts from the dashboard
  2. Send initial instructions to orient participants
  3. Monitor execution signals to track progress
  4. Send follow-up instructions based on signals

For additional support, please contact your system administrator.
