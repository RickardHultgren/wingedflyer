The 5 Bounded Support Offer Types
1. Adjust Payment Timing

Coordination: Change dates, meeting times, process adjustments

2. Share Information

Info Gap: Resources, contacts, programs they don't know about

3. Offer Restructuring

Shock Response: Payment restructuring for external shocks

4. Facilitate Service Access

Info/Constraint: Connect to complementary services

5. Request Conversation

Complex: For situations needing dialogue

All offers can be accepted, declined, or modified by borrowers.
