Welcome to Your Participant Portal

This portal allows you to manage your activities, send signals about your progress, and communicate with your {{=session.responsible_name}}.

Key Features:
  • Dashboard: Overview of your activities, signals, and instructions
  • Work Activities: Create and manage your activities or goals
  • Execution Signals: Report daily progress on your activities
  • Instructions: Receive and respond to messages from your responsible entity
  • Flyers: Create and publish public content
  • Profile: Manage your personal information
Getting Started:
  1. Create your work activities or goals
  2. Send regular execution signals to report progress
  3. Check your inbox regularly for instructions
  4. Respond to instructions that require responses
  5. Keep your profile information up to date
Understanding Execution Signals:

BETTER: Your execution exceeded your own expectations

AS EXPECTED: Your execution met your expectations

WORSE: Your execution fell short of your expectations

These signals help you and your responsible entity track progress honestly and adjust support as needed.

For additional support, please contact your responsible entity: {{=session.responsible_name}}
